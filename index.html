LuminaVibe Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

1. Introduction

LuminaVibe ("we", "our", "app") is a digital spiritual experience platform designed for entertainment, personal awareness, and symbolic interpretation. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of data:

  • Device information (model, OS version, language)
  • App usage analytics
  • Advertising identifiers (for AdMob)
  • Approximate location (non-precise, for ad personalization)
  • Firebase Analytics event data

We do not collect sensitive personal data such as financial information, passwords, or government IDs.

3. How We Use Data

  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Personalize content and spiritual readings
  • Display relevant advertisements (AdMob)
  • Analyze user behavior (Firebase Analytics)
  • Fix bugs and optimize system stability

4. Third-Party Services

LuminaVibe integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob – for advertising monetization
  • Google Firebase Analytics – for usage analytics and event tracking

These services may collect and process data according to their own privacy policies.

5. Advertising (AdMob)

The app uses Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may use advertising identifiers to serve personalized or non-personalized ads.

Users may opt out of personalized ads via device settings.

6. Firebase Analytics

We use Firebase Analytics to understand user interaction patterns, improve features, and optimize performance. Data collected is aggregated and anonymized where possible.

7. Permissions

The application may request the following permissions:

  • Internet access (for content, ads, analytics)
  • Network state access (performance optimization)
  • Notifications (daily tarot / spiritual updates)

8. Data Retention

Data is retained as long as necessary for analytics and app functionality. Users cannot directly request deletion unless supported by platform-level tools.

9. Security

We implement reasonable technical safeguards to protect collected data. However, no system is 100% secure.

10. Disclaimer

LuminaVibe is an entertainment-based application. All aura readings, tarot interpretations, and spiritual analyses are symbolic and not scientifically validated or medically accurate.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
